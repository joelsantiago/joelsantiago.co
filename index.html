Personal Profile

A brief bit about myself

My name is Joel Santiago and I'm a Bay Area, CA based software developer and web designer with a love for all things code. My specialties include C and Java programming along with a passion for responsive web design, aiming for a "mobile first" approach. One of the many new projects I'm currently working on is expanding my knowledge and experience in the field of mobile app development, namely iOS and Android.

I do most of my web design in Coda 2 from Panic, though I can't deny that Dreamweaver sneaks in every now and again. Being mostly Mac based, Xcode gets used quite often for any C/Obj-C development and while I'm plenty comfortable with Eclipse, most of my Java programming gets accomplished through IntelliJ IDEA, a wonderful IDE from JetBrains. Despite those great IDE's, I often find myself gravitating towards the awesome, yet simplistic Sublime Text Editor for many projects. I'm no stranger working within the confines of a command line; often preferring it's complexities to the standard GUI. I push to Github for version control.

When I'm not coding, you might find me taking in a movie, playing a game on Xbox, watching a baseball game, or attempting to clear out my Netflix queue. On the days that I'm up for something a little more active, I might grab the mountain bike and go for a ride, take the FJ Cruiser out for an excursion or attempt to improve my Phantom flying skills.
